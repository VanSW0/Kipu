Common Questions

How do I save a link?

Tap Share in any app and choose Kipu.

How do I restore purchases?

Open Kipu, go to the Pro screen, and tap Restore Purchases.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Open iPhone Settings > Apple Account > Subscriptions.

Where is my data stored?

Kipu stores your bookmark library locally on your device unless you choose to export or share it.

I lost access to Secret Vault. Can it be recovered?

If you lose your PIN, passphrase, or recovery information, Kipu may not be able to recover encrypted vault data.
